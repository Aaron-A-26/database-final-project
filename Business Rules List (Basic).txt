- One store (at a given moment) can have many customers.
- A store (at a given moment) may not have any customers.
- A store must be managed by at least one salesperson.
- A salesperson manages at most one store.
- A salesperson may not be assigned to manage a store.
- A salesperson manages at most one region.
- A salesperson may not be assigned to manage a region.
- A CEO can manage many salespeople.
- A CEO must manage at least one salesperson.
